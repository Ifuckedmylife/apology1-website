Final Apology

Honestly, I don't know what to write here. What words could I possibly use to ask for your forgiveness? I don't know. I just know how to speak my heart out and that's what I'll do.

The distance you ended up putting yourself at; this past month, it scared me. I still am scared.

Initially I had thought that I could wait for you. Wait a week, wait a month, wait a year. But I realized, I don't have that luxury. I only have a year and a half left with you. I was sad.

After all, I can make a thousand new friends but...how could they ever be you?

Your actions, your words they always lived where all the good ones did...in my heart 🤍

The hardest thing was finding each other in millions of people. Now, let us do the easier thing and never lose each other.

I want to be a part of the remainder of your college life.

I am in no position to ask you to do something but... will you join me in the library to study everyday from 16th July onwards? You don't need to talk, you don't need to say. Just study.

Spending some time together even if we don't speak, it'll mean a lot.

I'll wait for your answer.

I am sorry, Sindhu.
